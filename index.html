A place where curiosity grows and the outdoors becomes the classroom.

Step onto our East Legon campus and feel it immediately — this place sings. With a library, large play areas and room to roam, learning isn't contained to the classroom. Here, children sketch under trees, build friendships in the open air, and discover who they're becoming.

Rooted in East Legon

Just minutes from the heart of East Legon and close to MDS Lancet Labs, our campus is an easy, calm and secure place for families — giving children room to breathe while keeping them anchored to the energy of Accra.

Class extends beyond Herons

The whole city becomes a learning playground. From cultural visits to community service in Accra, children build a special relationship with their home — and find their place within it.

World citizens in the making

With British & Cambridge curricula, our learners are equipped with skills that match the changes of our global world — ready to connect, contribute and lead anywhere.
